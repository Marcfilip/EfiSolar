Who we are

Born in Brazil over 6 years ago as a company for renewable energies - focus on Energy efficiency and solar energy, EfiSolar has specialized in photovoltaic installations in Brazil since 2015. Our credo: Renewable energies are important and forward-looking. Everyone can and should take advantage of a photovoltaic system. The sun rises for everyone!
